Risk Score Nonrescale

The Relative Risk Score assigned to an individual that indicates the expected relative cost risk of the individual compared to the average in the time period, expressed on a scale of 100. Because the score is not rescaled to the population on which the DCGs were assigned, the average (100) represents the average for the nationwide dataset on which the model was developed. This score is produced by the DCG models and is available only within the DCG Incurred category.
